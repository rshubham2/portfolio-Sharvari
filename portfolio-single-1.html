Role Identity, Freelancer
Client unslate
Visit Drive Link

Designed an engaging t-shirt mockup featuring Anya Forger from "SPY x FAMILY" using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The front design showcases Anya with a playful expression, creatively integrating the Nike swoosh, while the back design highlights her love for peanuts with vibrant illustrations and text. This project blends anime aesthetics with brand elements, resulting in a unique and appealing merchandise concept.

This design showcases Anya Forger's playful personality and love for peanuts, creatively integrating iconic elements from the anime with recognizable brand symbols. The front features Anya's cheerful expression alongside the Nike swoosh, while the back highlights a whimsical peanut illustration. This project demonstrates a seamless blend of anime aesthetics and branding, resulting in a unique and eye-catching piece of merchandise.
